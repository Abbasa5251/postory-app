## 1. Product overview

**One-liner:** A team-based SaaS where small brand agencies generate social posts (copy + AI images), route them through internal and client approval, and schedule them to 6 platforms вЂ” one workspace per client brand вЂ” then prove the results with client-ready analytics.

**Positioning:** Not "cheaper Hootsuite." The pitch is the *closed loop small agencies actually run*: brief в†’ on-brand AI content в†’ internal review в†’ client approves from their phone в†’ auto-publish в†’ monthly client report. Competitors do pieces; we do the loop.

### 1.1 Personas
- **Agency owner** (better-auth `owner`) вЂ” billing, seats, brands, everything.
- **Account manager** (`admin` / custom `approver`) вЂ” reviews, approves, schedules, owns client relationships.
- **Content creator** (custom `creator`) вЂ” drafts, generates, submits; sees only assigned brands.
- **Client stakeholder (external, no account)** вЂ” approves posts and views reports via tokenized portal links.

### 1.2 Goals
- Routine post: brief в†’ scheduled in < 5 min.
- Zero posts published without required approvals (internal, and client where enabled).
- Publish reliability в‰Ґ 99.5% (beta), 99.9% (GA).
- Blended gross margin в‰Ґ 55% by month 6 post-GA (rising with Zernio band mix).

### 1.3 Non-goals (v1)
AI video generation (post-launch, D4) В· X/Twitter, Pinterest, Bluesky, Reddit, etc. (post-launch platforms) В· social listening В· paid ads В· unified DM inbox В· multi-step approval chains В· white-label portal domains В· native mobile apps В· free plan.

## 3. Architecture decision records

- [ ] **ADR-001 вЂ” Our DB is the source of truth for posts.** Zernio post created only at schedule time; `zernio_post_id` stored; status driven by webhooks + 15-min reconciliation sweep. Zernio isolated behind a `publishing/` module.
- [ ] **ADR-002 (rev) вЂ” Tenancy via better-auth organizations.** Org = agency. `organization`, `member`, `invitation`, `session` tables owned by better-auth migrations in our Postgres. Active org read from `session.activeOrganizationId`. **Every domain table carries `org_id` FK в†’ better-auth `organization.id`; every query flows through a data-access layer requiring `orgId`.** No webhook mirroring (Clerk's Epic-A4 deleted). This DAL is now the tenant-isolation *security boundary* вЂ” see NFR + authz test suite. *(2026-07-15: better-auth org tables live in Neon; active-org set on sign-in/create/accept verified. DAL itself pending вЂ” A5.)*
- [ ] **ADR-003 вЂ” All generation & publishing async via Inngest.** Actions enqueue and return job ids; workers do the work; UI polls job status (2s while live). No AI/publish call in a request handler.
- [ ] **ADR-004 вЂ” Direct Stripe.** Tier products (monthly + annual) + credit-pack one-off prices + `$9/mo` extra-account subscription item. Entitlements mirrored to `subscriptions` table; hot-path gates read our DB.
- [ ] **ADR-005 вЂ” Append-only credit ledger.** Reserve before generation в†’ settle/refund after. Balance = materialized SUM per org.
- [ ] **ADR-006 вЂ” Webhook ingestion.** Verify signature в†’ insert `webhook_events` (unique on provider event id) в†’ 200 fast в†’ process via Inngest. Applies to Stripe and Zernio (OpenRouter's Image API is synchronous request/response вЂ” no webhook; revisit if async video jobs need one post-launch).
- [ ] **ADR-007 вЂ” Media pipeline.** OpenRouter image output (base64) в†’ decode в†’ moderation в†’ R2 (`org/{orgId}/brand/{brandId}/вЂ¦`) в†’ CDN; public URLs handed to Zernio at publish.
- [ ] **ADR-008 вЂ” Client portal is token-based.** Signed, expiring, revocable tokens; scoped to approval sets *or* report views (same shell, two capabilities). All portal actions audited.
- [x] **ADR-009 (new; amended 2026-07-17, B1 grill; re-amended 2026-07-17, B3 grill вЂ” R1 resolved; implemented 2026-07-18 in B3, PR #32 + #33) вЂ” Brand в†” Zernio profile is 1:1, profile provisioned lazily.** A brand owns exactly **one** Zernio profile, but the `zernio_profiles` row and the Zernio `POST /api/v1/profiles` call are created **on first account placement (B3), not at brand creation (B1)** вЂ” a brand with zero connected accounts has no Zernio profile at all. This keeps brand creation a pure local mutation with no external dependency (Zernio being slow/down can't fail or slow a brand create). Invisible to users; `social_accounts.zernio_profile_id` records placement. The former overflow-profile mechanism (`profile_no` 2, 3, вЂ¦ auto-created on same-platform duplicates) is therefore **removed** and Brandв†”profile collapses to 1:1 (a brand's one profile holds all its accounts, including multiples of the same platform). Docs are strong-but-not-conclusive on the exact same-platform-duplicate edge в†’ a one-line Zernio support confirmation is out (Epic B carry-over); the overflow codepath is not physically deleted from any lingering references until support replies, but no new code should assume >1 profile per brand.
